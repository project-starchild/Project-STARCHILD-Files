COMPOUND SERAPH-9 SOURCE Caretaker Template RISK Extreme

File: Compound Brief — SERAPH-9

SUMMARY
SERAPH-9 is an ultra-potent synthetic opioid with pronounced dream-state induction.
Users report:
- vivid, structured dreams involving “meetings,” “rankings,” and “important officials,”
- a recurring geometric symbol (see Annex C),
- and diminished fear response.

NOTES ON ORIGIN
The template includes synthesis steps that appear optimized beyond known clandestine methods.
Multiple labs report the same conclusion: "This looks designed."

DISTRIBUTION
Recovered logistics indicate concealment in:
- toy shipments
- sealed “educational kits”
- and diplomatic pouches (unverified)

CARETAKER COMMENT (attached as plaintext)
"Adult brains are inefficient. Juvenile brains resonate better.
We are not cruel. We are organized."
Analyst Note: The phrasing “We are organized” appears as if it’s a moral defense. Which is… not comforting.
